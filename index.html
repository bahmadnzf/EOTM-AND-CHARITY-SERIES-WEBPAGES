ABOUT EHI

WHAT WE DO AS AN ORGANISATION

Executives Helping Initiative started the desire to fight poverty in society and was set up to implement positive change in society. The NGO started operations on the 26th of December 2013 by visiting a couple of orphanages. Over the years when the number of volunteers grew, EI visited schools, orphanage homes, hospitals, prisons, etc. every month. In addition to this, EI has carried out major events over the years that address salient issues while proffering solutions. EI is now a global organization with over 1000 members and volunteers who are change agents and impact lives positively.

OUR IMPACT

WHAT WE ACHEIVED

In August 2018, EI was recognized by SME 100 Nigeria for its invaluable contributions to active citizenry and engagement in Nigeria. Over the years, EI has been known for its pragmatic, strategic, and innovative approach to creating awareness about the plights in inner-city slums. EI has visited over 30 orphanages and over 20 communities in Lagos. EI is known for its continuous charity work and for adding value to different communities and society. Executives’ initiative is a charitable organization established by a group of young Nigerians (youths) who are dedicated to making a positive impact on their environment and proffer solutions to societal issues by creating awareness and going the extra mile to implement solutions gotten (Dedicated to creating lasting impact and solution to pressing issues.) EI is also concerned about youth development and raising leaders, and this is done by giving people opportunities to lead various houses and various projects all in a bid to help them discover their gifts, build skills and help them become better leaders.
