WHAT WE DO

The Next Generation of Change Agents

Founded in 2013, Executives Helping Initiative is a youth-led movement of fighting poverty through action. At the end of Eevery month, we show up at orphanages, prisons, hospitals, and underserved communities across Lagos—delivering food, medical care, hope, and second chances.

We don't just raise awareness. We implement solutions.

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Dare Asobele

Founder & Visionary

Lawyer Project Manager Change Catalyst

Timothy-Asobele Emmanuel Dare—known simply as Dare Asobele. What sparked this journey? Anger turned into action. Dare founded Executives Helping Initiative in 2013 as a law student at the University of Lagos after visiting an orphanage and realizing good intentions weren't enough. Inspired by legal giants Chief Gani Fawehinmi and Judge Chukwudifu Oputa, he rallied five friends and turned compassion into a national movement.

Called to the Nigerian Bar (2015) | PMP® Certified

Also founded ASADS (Anti-Suicide and Depression Squad)

The world just needs people willing to take action.
