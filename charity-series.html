The Charity Series

The Charity Series is an annual three-part program by Executives Helping Initiative (EHI), designed to raise funds, transform lives, and bring hope to communities.The Charity Series is a flagship program within EHI designed to respond to urgent needs and uplift lives with strategic, compassionate action. It sits at the heart of our End of the Month Outreach — a signature initiative carried out at the close of each month.

Founded on the 26th of December 2013, EHI began by visiting orphanages. Over the years, as our volunteer family grew to over 1,000 strong, the reach expanded to Prisons, schools, hospitals, and inner-city communities every month. The Charity Series is the vehicle through which compassion becomes concrete, meaningful action.

"These moments reflect who we are: people helping people with purpose, presence, and love."

Recognised by SME 100 Nigeria in 2018 for invaluable contributions to active citizenry, EHI has grown into a global organisation. But our monthly outreach commitment remains unchanged — because those who need help cannot wait.

What We Do — Five Pillars

01

Funraiser Night

▼

Raising funds for cancer,sickle cell, and mental health and joy — restoring hope and showing every child they are deeply valued.

02

Community Impacts

▼

Our teams step into underserved communities — over 20 in Lagos alone — with direct engagement and tangible support for residents who need it most.

03

Prison Impact

▼

We plan Feeding, supporting, and encouraging inmates bringing what they lack with the little we offer — providing check-ups, welfare, and medical supplies.

04

Healthcare Support

▼

From medicine donations to health awareness campaigns, we advocate for good health and bring care closer to those who cannot easily access it.
