What Is EOTM?

Turning Compassion into Action

End of the Month Outreach is EHI's signature community support initiative, carried out at the close of each month. It is a series of impactful visits and community engagements designed to uplift, support, and bring hope to underserved communities across Nigeria.

The Charity Series is a flagship program within EHI designed to respond to urgent needs and uplift lives with strategic, compassionate action. Through these visits, we step beyond intention — not just with words, but with action.

These moments reflect who we are: people helping people with purpose, presence, and love.
